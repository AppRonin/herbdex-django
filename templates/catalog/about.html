About Herbdex

A digital apothecary and searchable herb catalog.

What is Herbdex?

Herbdex is a searchable catalog of medicinal herbs, created as a digital apothecary to organize and store knowledge about natural remedies, traditional uses, and plant-based treatments.

The goal is simple: make it easy to look up herbs by name or by illness/medical use, and keep structured notes about what each herb can do.
